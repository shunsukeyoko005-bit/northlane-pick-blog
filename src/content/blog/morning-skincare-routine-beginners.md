# Morning Skincare Routine for Beginners (5 Simple Steps)

Skincare looks complicated from the outside — shelves of serums, ten-step routines, conflicting advice. But a great morning routine really only needs a handful of products in the right order. Here's a beginner-friendly AM routine you can actually stick to.

## The 5-step morning routine

1. **Cleanse** — remove overnight oil and reset your skin
2. **Treat (serum)** — one targeted active, optional but nice
3. **Moisturize** — hydrate and protect the skin barrier
4. **Protect (SPF)** — the most important anti-aging step, every single day
5. **(Optional) Makeup** — goes on top of fully set sunscreen

The golden rule for layering: **thinnest to thickest**. Watery products first, creams and SPF last.

## Beginner tips

- **Start slow** — add one product at a time so you know what works
- **Consistency beats complexity** — the same simple routine daily wins
- **Patch test** new actives on your jaw before going all-in
- **Give each step 30–60 seconds** to absorb before the next
